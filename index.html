Admissions Information

Classes Offered

Class 1st to 10th: We offer admissions for students from Class 1st to 10th.

CBSE and State Board Sections: We have separate sections for CBSE and State Board curriculum, allowing parents to choose the educational board that best suits their child's needs.

Admission Process

Application Form: Interested parents/guardians can obtain the admission application form from the school office or download it from our website.

Submission: Completed application forms should be submitted to the school office along with the required documents.

Entrance Test (if applicable): Depending on the class and availability, students may be required to appear for an entrance test.

Interview: Shortlisted candidates will be called for an interview with school authorities.

Admission Confirmation: Upon successful completion of the admission process, parents will receive confirmation of admission along with further instructions.

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous School Records (for transfer students)
  • Passport-sized Photographs
  • Address Proof
  • Aadhar Card or any other Identification Proof
